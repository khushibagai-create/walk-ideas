Walk Experiment Framework

5 experiments. 1000 users. One winner. Each concept is being tested with 200 members to find which mechanic drives the highest Day 7 & Day 30 retention.

Experiment B
Habit Loop
Intention → reminder → walk → close the loop ("what did you notice?")
Hypothesis: Intention + reflection creates the strongest habit loop

Experiment C
Walk Partners
Your score is based on whether your partner walked
Hypothesis: Social obligation (partner's score = your walk) drives highest consistency
→
👥
Experiment D
Friend Groups
Friends together — 5/6 walked today. Cooperative within, competitive between groups
Hypothesis: Group accountability (5/6 today) is more motivating than individual
→
🏆
Experiment E
Milestone Cards
Strava-style shareable achievement cards — habit consistency + community belonging
Hypothesis: Shareable achievements drive both retention and viral referrals
